Listen with your "3rd Ear"

As you would look with your “3rd eye” do the same when listening. How we hear sound is just as important as the sound produced.
Ask yourself several questions when listening:
1) Who is producing the sound?
2) How do you feel about what you’ve heard?
3) Why do you feel that way?

-these questions serve as a guide for practicing non-judgmental listening
-respect differences in dialect, diction, and accent
-acknowledge your biases when listening to a person
-make sure listening is active, engage
