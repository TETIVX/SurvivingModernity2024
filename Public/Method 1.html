Communal Thinking

This survival technique incorporates communication, building bonds, and developing trust. To succeed, you will need to make decisions with the thought of others in mind. Realize that decisions you make impact your community, which is your family.

Quick Tips!

  • Include your partner, friends, and other community members in long-term planning.
  • consider feelings, goals, and personal experiences of those close to you as well as those you’re still familiarizing yourself with
  • challenge yourself with tasks like reaching out to those you don’t know; giving a few bucks here-and-there; and encourage all around you
  • Expand your circle—establish many trusting relationships, building communal trust.
  • Include. Never exclude.
