WRITE...EVERYTHING!

The importance of writing can’t be emphasized enough; this tool affords you unlimited opportunities at crafting history; exploring innermost thoughts and emotions; finding an internal balance; and this will probably be the best way for future generations to connect with you and your era.

Helpful hints

  • Take pictures, record sounds, scan notes; create reference points for yourself
  • Analyze, rewrite, re-analyze, evaluate, interpret, revise, keep going…
  • These records and accounts ensure the survival of you, your community, and legacy
  • This makes change the power structure if the right person is inspired by what it is that you experienced and wrote.
