Reject the Binary

Black Atlantic Religion encompasses the true essence of fluidity, allowing the recycling and retention of West African religious practices. This concept involves realization of change, progress, and growth, or whatever you would like to call it. Understand that life is relative, categorizing compromises the experience.

  • There are no leaders and followers…everyone experiences both at one point and multiple points in their lives.
  • Don’t succumb to culture of labeling, categorization, and grouping
  • constantly challenge the assumptions of modernity; there may be more than one or two of anything
  • understand that you are an existing and evolving continuum
